Simple Daily Changes for a Greener Planet

Adopting eco-friendly habits is a small step toward a healthier environment. Here are 10 simple habits you can start today:

  1. Use a reusable water bottle.
  2. Bring your own shopping bags.
  3. Reduce food waste by planning meals.
  4. Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use.
  5. Use public transport, bike, or walk instead of driving.
  6. Buy local and organic produce.
  7. Avoid single-use plastics.
  8. Recycle properly and compost organic waste.
  9. Switch to energy-efficient appliances.
  10. Support eco-conscious brands.

Every small habit adds up to a big impact. Start today!
